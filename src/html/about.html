About the DIVA app

Welcome to The DIVA App, your digital assistant! Its mission is to empower you with accurate, real-time weather and disaster updates whether you're at home or on the go. The DIVA App delivers a seamless experience, ensuring you're prepared for whatever the weather brings.

Core Features:

  • Real-Time Disaster Alerts
  • Weather Updates
  • Disaster Preparedness Guides
  • Virtual Assistance
  • Emergency Tools
  • Disaster Response Guidance
  • Education and Awareness

User Experience:

  • User-friendly interface designed for simplicity and ease of use.
  • Responsive design compatible with various devices and screen sizes.
  • Dark mode feature to reduce eye strain and save device battery life.
  • Customizable settings to personalize your weather data display.

Our Commitment:

With DIVA, we are more than an app we are your partner in safety, your guide in times of uncertainty, and your source of hope in the face of adversity. Together, let’s build a safer, more prepared environment.
